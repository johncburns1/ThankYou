Thank You


Infoblox Tacoma Employees,

I want to thank you all for granting me the opportunity to interview
for an internship position at Infoblox in Tacoma. Getting to talk with
you guys about Infoblox’s network security and management tools has allowed
me to glean a more concrete understanding of how cybersecurity is evolving.

I was nervous about my interview, but you all made the experience an enjoyable and interesting one.
Upon my arrival at the Infoblox office, I was promptly greeted by Meghann, Jennifer,
and their collective hospitality. Although I brought my own water bottle
and was not in need of any additional refreshments, the multitude of beverage
offers helped to quell my anxieties. The level of engagement that I
received from Minh, Brian, Daniel, and David during my interview(s)
was—for lack of a better word—awesome. I left the office knowing more than I did when I arrived there.

The Infoblox work environment seems competitive,
stimulating, and full of smart, kind, and passionate individuals. My hope is that
you all strive for excellence and continuing to pass your wisdom down to those willing to learn.

--John "Jack" Burns
